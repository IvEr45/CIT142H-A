About Me

Hey, I'm Ivereson Ferasan, a self-taught digital artist passionate about transforming ideas into vibrant visual narratives. Specializing in portrait painting, I bring emotions and feelings to life through digital painting, illustration, and character design. My artistic journey spans 3 years of continuous exploration and refinement, embracing the dynamic world of Krita to create captivating artwork. Fueled by a love for painting, each piece is a chapter in my ongoing learning adventure. Open for commissions, I'm excited to collaborate and bring your concepts to the digital canvas. Let's connect through social media by pressing the contact me tab in this website and embark on a creative journey together!
